Link Title

Is there a way to predict if an online article is clickbait based on structure and use of the title?

Learning to sift through the deluge of information in the modern world is rapidly becoming a required skill and determining truth in phrasing is important in both artifical intelligence as well as maximizing a person's precious time.

Theories

  1. There is a particular pattern used to mask clickbat - this implies that we as humans have a pre-desposition to certain communication patterns.
  2. If article sources can be assigned a co-efficient, implying that we can rank article sources by "trustworthiness"
  3. Which cognitive distortions/logical fallicies are most exploited in the creation of clickbait.
